# Health & Fitness Documentation

This directory contains the public-facing documentation for the Health & Fitness project. These are the curated guides and plans shared on the portfolio.

## Contents

- **fitness-roadmap.md** - Your phased progression plan for achieving long-term fitness goals, from foundational work through advanced training
- **Full-Meal-Plan.md** - Comprehensive nutrition strategy designed for sustainable health improvement
- **Shopping-List-and-Estimate.md** - Practical grocery list with cost estimates based on the meal plan

## Using These Documents

These files form the public-facing portfolio content for the Health & Fitness project. They demonstrate your approach to fitness planning and nutrition strategy.

For working logs and data, see the `data/` directory (not included in public portfolio).

## Updates

These documents are living guides that evolve as your knowledge and experience grow. They represent your current best practices and can be refined as you gain new insights.
