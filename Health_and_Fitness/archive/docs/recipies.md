## Breakfast Burritos (makes 12)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb sausage (Tennessee Pride mild)
- 1 can Rotel (10 oz, original)
- 12 Soft Taco Tortillas (Mission)
- 24 tbsp Medium Chunky Salsa (Pace)
- 6 slices American cheese (Kraft Singles)
- 18 medium eggs

**Per Burrito Nutritional Info:**
- **Calories:** 380-420 cal
- **Protein:** 16-18g
- **Fat:** 20-23g
- **Carbs:** 28-32g
- **Fiber:** 1-2g

**Ingredient Breakdown (per burrito):**
- Sausage (1.3 oz): 110 cal, 5g protein, 9g fat
- Egg (1.5 medium): 110 cal, 13g protein, 8g fat
- Tortilla: 160 cal, 5g protein, 4g fat, 28g carbs
- Cheese (0.5 slice): 45 cal, 3g protein, 4g fat
- Rotel + Salsa: 15-25 cal, <1g protein

## Frozen Meal on Spinach

**Ingredients:**
- 3 cups raw spinach
- 3 servings Skillet Meals, Three Cheese Chicken (Birds Eye)

**Per Serving Nutritional Info:**
- **Calories:** 280-320 cal
- **Protein:** 22-26g
- **Fat:** 12-15g
- **Carbs:** 18-22g
- **Fiber:** 2-3g

**Ingredient Breakdown (per serving):**
- Spinach (1 cup raw): 7 cal, 1g protein, 0.1g fat, 1g carbs, 0.7g fiber
- Birds Eye Skillet Meal (1 serving): 270-300 cal, 21-25g protein, 12-14g fat, 17-21g carbs, 1.5g fiber
